# Future Doctrine Lesson Ideas

These are the remaining topic ideas from the May 22, 2026 review of the existing lesson archive. They are parked here for future brunch planning.

## Doctrine of Salvation: God's Rescue Plan

Big idea: sin is the problem; Jesus is God's solution.

Key passages: Romans 6:23, Ephesians 2:8-9, John 3:16, 2 Corinthians 5:21.

Age fit:
- Jadon: rescue story.
- Mya: grace means you cannot earn, fake, or perform your way into being loved by God.

## Repentance and Faith: How We Respond to the Gospel

Big idea: repentance is turning from sin; faith is trusting Jesus, not yourself.

Key passages: Mark 1:15, Acts 16:31, Luke 15, James 2:17.

Age fit:
- Jadon: turning around when you are going the wrong direction.
- Mya: the difference between feeling bad, changing behavior, and actually surrendering.

## The Holy Spirit: God With Us and In Us

Big idea: Christians do not obey God alone; the Spirit helps, convicts, comforts, teaches, and changes us.

Key passages: John 14:16-17, Galatians 5:22-23, Romans 8:9-14, Ephesians 1:13-14.

Age fit:
- Jadon: helper, coach, guide.
- Mya: conviction versus shame, fruit versus performance.

## The Church: Why Christians Need Each Other

Big idea: God saves people into a family/body, not isolated individuals.

Key passages: Acts 2:42-47, 1 Corinthians 12:12-27, Hebrews 10:24-25, Ephesians 4:11-16.

Age fit:
- Jadon: body parts working together.
- Mya: community, accountability, using gifts, and why "I can follow Jesus alone" does not match the Bible.

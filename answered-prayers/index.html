The Airbnb Host Came Through at the Last Minute

Fifteen minutes from the rental, no response from the host, and no way in. We prayed. He responded within a minute.

10 Seats on the Flight Home

Missing Bags Found Before Tracking Them Down

After the break-in, we prayed we'd find the stolen items before having to track the "Find My" location ourselves — which could have meant walking into something dangerous. We found the bags on our own first. A situation that could have turned threatening was quietly avoided.

Answered Prayer

The Last Flight Out

Severe weather was wreaking havoc on flights across the board. We prayed our plane would make it in safely — and that the crew delays wouldn't stack up so badly that they'd be grounded before we could depart. God answered. Our plane arrived, the crew made it work, and we lifted off — on the last flight out.
